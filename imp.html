ASIN PROGRESSION(Amazon standard identification number)

Do follow the same order mentioned below

    1.LPN

    2.X00

    3.B00

    4.Retail SKU

    5.ISBN(vendor provided barcod)

    6.UPC(vendor provided barcode)

    7.EAN(vendor provided barcode)

BIN Etiquette(B.O.A.T.S)

  • BLOCKING
  • OVERHANGING/OVERSTUFFED
  • ABOVE THE BIN DIVIDER
  • TYPE OF BIN
  • SIMILAR SAME ASIN

MASTER PACK LABLE?

Pack of items which need to be separated and pick the number of given quantity as shown in Scanner (device)

TIP: Check the weight, whether its matching according to the given product

MASTER SET LABLE?

Pack of items which need not to be separated and pick the set of items according to the given quantity

TIP: Check the weight, whether its matching according to the given product

OPERATIONAL SAFETY RULE

  • Only push the Cart at walking speed
  • Maintain two meters apart
  • Don't obstruct your line of sight
  • At Corner's, Pause , Check, Shout Corner
  • Park Carts in designated area
  • Maintain control with both hands on the cart
