SAFETY

Why do we need to follow Safety Tips?

For absence of Harm, Accidents, and injuries we need to follow "SAFETY" Tips

Safety tips we should know

  • Wear safety "Hand Gloves".
  • Don't use"CELL PHONE".
  • Always shout "CORNER" at the corner points.
  • Use "Step Ladders" for all picks above shoulder height.
  • Wear "Safety Shoes" while moving the Pallets.
  • Don't run on "floor".
  • Move "Single Cart"at a time.
  • Do follow 5'S.
  • If you find waste items on floor, like plastics and empty carton box ,pick and put it in a trash bin.
  • Only push the cart(trolley) at walking speed.
  • NOTE: Do not work if you're unsafe as SAFETY is our first priority.
