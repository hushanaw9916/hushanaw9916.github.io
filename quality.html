QUALITY

Why do we need to follow Quality tips?

Quality is the degree to which something meets requirements, standards, or expectations to run the work smoothly.

Quality tips for PICK

  • Items which are found on floor,pick and put it in the"Amnesty bin"(blue bin).
  • Always check six sides of the items, If the item is damaged,Go to menu bar and mark it as damged item and place it inside the damage bin(red bin) and scan the barcode which is on the DAMAGE bin(red bin).
  • Always check six sides of the items and scan the "right barcode" according to the "ASIN"progression and as per required barcode shown in system.
  • Avoid "Put back" error (“Items that are removed from a bin, if not picked, should be placed back into the same bin”).
  • Reject the item only if the barcode is Unscannable.
  • Only if the item is physically not present in the bin, GO to menu and select "missing item" and Scan all items which are in bin".
  • Put the Right item in Right Tote
  • Shake the fragile item lightly,To know whether the item is damged or not and then pick the item if its not damaged
  • Don't over fill the Tote.
  • Place the items safely in tote to avoid Damage.
  • Strictly follow the instructions which you receive in the "Message Box".
